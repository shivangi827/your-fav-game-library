Bounty of the Lost

Explore. Collect. Escape alive.

How to Play

  • Click adjacent tiles to explore the dungeon
  • Find and collect 3 Soul Shards guarded by powerful enemies
  • Numbers on tiles show nearby enemies (like Minesweeper)
  • Each shard makes escape harder — your vision darkens and you take damage moving
  • Return to the center Hub to extract with your shards
  • New dungeon every day — same layout for everyone!
